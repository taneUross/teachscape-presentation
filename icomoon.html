Our Icon Strategy

  • While icons may be attractive, they are oftentimes poor communicator so reduce its use.
  • All icons should be documented on this page and should be used consistently.
  • All icons come from our custom IcoMoon. No exception.
  • Icons should have a distinct shape and integrate with the Teachscape icon logos.

Icons Not to Use

  • Printer to indicate print
  • Envelope to indicate contact/mail
